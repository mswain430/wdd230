Blanding Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Domain Name

blandingchamber.com

Site Name

Blanding Chamber of Commerce

Site Description

An important part of the chamber is to attract members, business Owners who would like to promote their business.

The Blanding Chamber provides a Business Directory so residents or visitors can learn more about local businesses.

The Blanding Chamber provides learning opportunities. Either through local businesses who want to share what they do.

The Chamber features speakers who can teach others how to develop their businesses.

The Blanding Chamber is an advocacy group. We promote local culture and local business.

Purpose Statement

The Blanding Chamber of Commerce...

  • Promotes member businesses by providing educational programs, networking, and advertising opportunities.

  • Creates a strong local economy by advocating for business, and developing partnerships with community groups and government agencies.

Personas

Member Spotlight: Dr. Matthew Culerson, Blanding Integrative Medicine & Spa

Age: 45, Gender: Male, status: married with a family of four.

Dr Culterson has recently established his business in Blanding and has added a Spa. He wants to promote Health and Wellness in the Community.

Dr Culerson would like to join the chamber so he can become a viable member of the community and give back. He want to promote his business. He wants to do this by advertising on the Chamber. He also wants to introduce Integrative medicine and feature his Spa.

He is a business owner willing to share his knowledge with the community. He wants to advertise and even help sponsor the chamber

He's interested in Networking Opportunities.

He wants to get involved in the community.

Share expertise in developing a business.

Learn about others who have been successful in business.

Spotlight: Janice Ott, school teacher and Local Resident

Age: 27, Gender: Female, Status: single

Janice would like to know more about the happenings in the community. She enjoys hiking and visiting the national parks. She would like to do this in a group setting.

She also likes to visit the local stores to get her hair and nails done. She loves to shop and socialize with the business owners.

She has an interest in the Native Americans that once lived locally. She loves jewelry and would like to know where to find this.

She wants to take her class to the Dinasaur museum.

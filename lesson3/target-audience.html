Local Resident

Our site will feature a business directory for local residents to find businesses they can frequent locally.

A local resident would come to our site to learn about the community or find resources that may help them acclimate.

Business owners would promote special events and local residents would be able to see the calendar and attend these events. These events may promote heath and wellness or problem-solving issues like how to find a plumber.

Local Residents will have opportunities to either learn more about locals promoting their businesses or learn how to develop their own businesses.
